The **Initial Processing QA** (often referred to as **QC1**) is the first human gatekeeper in the workflow, where a [[Quality Checker (QC)]] validates the integrity of the raw and standardized audio. The checker uses a specific pass/fail checklist to identify "deal-breakers" like digital clipping, excessive background hiss, crosstalk between microphones, or speakers failing to follow the bilingual language ratio requirements. If the audio fails this stage, the session is flagged and routed back to the Recording node; if it passes, it is cleared for the noise augmentation phase.
