The **Noise Processing QA** (or **QC2**) is the final technical audit before the session moves to the [[ Annotator (AN)]]. During this stage, the [[Quality Checker (QC)]] ensures that the noise injection was performed correctly and that the speech remains intelligible despite the background interference. The checker verifies that the SNR levels match the project specs and that no artifacts or glitches were introduced during the mixing process. Successfully passing QC2 "locks" the audio files, signaling that the media is technically perfect and ready for full linguistic transcription and tagging.
