New speakers complete a 4-step onboarding wizard with a visual progress indicator showing completion across steps.

#### Step 1 — Signup
The speaker creates their account with:
- Email address (read-only, pre-filled from invitation)
- Full name (required)
- Password and confirm password (minimum 8 characters, with show/hide toggle)
- A preview of pre-assigned languages from the invitation (editable in next step)

Validation checks for empty name, short password, and password mismatch.

![[signup_page.png]]

#### Step 2 — Languages
The speaker selects their working languages:
- Language A (Main) — required, selected from 12 language options (EN-US, ES-MX, FR-FR, etc.)
- Language B (Secondary) — optional, shows all languages except the selected Language A
- Additional Languages — multi-select toggle buttons for all remaining languages
- A preview box shows the final language configuration

![[languages_page.png]]

#### Step 3 — Profile Information
- Timezone selection (13 options from America to Australia)
- Country selection (20 countries)
- Gender selection (Male, Female, Non-binary, Prefer not to say)
- Age range selection via interactive buttons (18–30, 30–50, 50+)

The Next button is disabled until all four fields are completed.

![[create_profile_page.png]]

#### Step 4 — Availability

The speaker sets their weekly availability using the same editor as the standalone Availability page (7 day rows with enable/disable toggles and time pickers). At least one day must be enabled to complete onboarding.

![[set_availability_page.png]]
#### Success Screen
A congratulations screen with an animated checkmark confirms the setup is complete. A 'Go to Sessions' button navigates to the sessions page.
