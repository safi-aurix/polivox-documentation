[[Speaker (SP)]] are the recording participants who produce bilingual audio content. They have the most interactive experience in the platform — browsing live topics, joining waiting lobbies, participating in timed recording sessions, managing their availability, and completing an onboarding process. The Speaker role also has a personal profile page and a comprehensive dashboard.
