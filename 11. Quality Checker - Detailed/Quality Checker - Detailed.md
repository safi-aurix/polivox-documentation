[[Quality Checker (QC)]] perform two distinct stages of audio quality review. QC1 reviews raw processed audio for technical quality before noise processing. QC2 reviews audio after noise injection to ensure the added noise layer hasn't degraded speech quality or intelligibility. Both stages share a similar workflow: review a queue, open sessions, use checklists and waveform tools, then approve or flag issues.
