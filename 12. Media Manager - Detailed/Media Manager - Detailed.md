[[Media Manager (MM)]] handle audio processing at two stages of the pipeline. First, they merge and normalize raw speaker tracks into a clean mixed recording ('Initial Processing'). Then, for 2-speaker projects, they inject synthetic environmental noise into the approved audio ('Noise Processing'). Both stages include tools for waveform editing, level adjustment, silence trimming, and previewing the final output before submitting to QC.
