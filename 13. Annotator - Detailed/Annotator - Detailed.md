[[Annotator (AN)]] work on transcription and linguistic annotation of QC-approved audio sessions. They produce turn-by-turn transcriptions with speaker identification, language tags, and precise timing information. The annotation interface includes audio playback, a reference transcript, and a full turn editor with feedback integration for QA review loops.
