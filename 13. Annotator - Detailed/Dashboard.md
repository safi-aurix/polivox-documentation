#### Statistics Cards
- **Total Assigned** — All sessions assigned to the annotator across all statuses
- **Pending** — Sessions awaiting initial annotation
- **Issues** — Sessions sent back by QA with feedback requiring revision
- **Completed** — Sessions that have passed QA and moved to delivery

#### Annotation Queue
A card shows the top 5 sessions in the annotation queue with topic, project, languages, speakers, duration, and status (Pending, Issues, or Resubmitted). A 'View all' link navigates to the full task page.
