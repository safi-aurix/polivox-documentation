Annotator QA reviewers verify the accuracy and completeness of annotations produced by Annotators. They serve as the final quality gate before sessions become ready for export.

**Responsibilities**
- Review transcriptions and annotations for accuracy and completeness
- Verify speaker identification, language labels, and timing accuracy
- Approve annotations that meet quality standards
- Request fixes with specific feedback when issues are found, linking to problematic turns
