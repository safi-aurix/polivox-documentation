The Project Manager has the highest-level view of the platform and oversees the entire data production lifecycle. They are the only role that can create and edit projects, manage team composition, and export final deliverables.

**Responsibilities**
- Create and configure projects (name, description, language pairs, topics, workflow)
- Assign speakers and staff to projects from the platform database
- Monitor session progress through the entire production pipeline
- Review sessions that have been flagged with issues at any QC or annotation stage.
- Make disposition decisions: send sessions for re-recording, reprocessing, or advance to next phase
- Evaluate speaker performance with star ratings and optional flagging
- Export completed and approved sessions to Google Drive
- Manage the speaker and staff databases (add, edit, delete, rate, flag). See details in [[Speaker & Staff Management]]
