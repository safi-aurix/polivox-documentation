Speakers are the recording participants who produce bilingual audio content. They browse available topics, join recording sessions with partner speakers, and can manage their availability and profile.

**Responsibilities**
- Complete the onboarding process (account setup, language selection, profile info, availability)
- Browse live topics and join recording sessions with assigned partners
- Speak naturally in both languages following topic prompts
- Ensure microphone quality and minimize background noise
- Complete full recording duration (minimum 5 minutes per session)
- Re-record sessions if flagged by the QC team
- Set and update weekly availability hours

**Available Pages**

| **Page**     | **Path**      | **Purpose**                                                                      |
| ------------ | ------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Dashboard    | /dashboard    | Personal stats, profile card, live topics, upcoming sessions                     |
| Sessions     | /sessions     | Browse live topics, join waiting lobby, enter recording room, view past sessions |
