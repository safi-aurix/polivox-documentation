Clicking a session row opens a detailed modal with three view modes based on session state:

#### 1. Active View
- Current pipeline status indicator showing which phase the session is in
- Assigned staff information (name, ID, role)
- Individual speaker audio tracks (collapsible, with audio player per speaker)
- Mixed audio track (highlighted center focus)

![[active_sesssion_detail.png]]

#### 2. Review View (Sessions with Issues)
- Review messages from QC/Annotation QA staff with their name, ID, role, and feedback text
- Mixed audio track for PM review
- Transcript viewer (if annotation-issues status)
- Individual speaker tracks for detailed review

**PM Actions:**
- **Send For Re-Recording** — Select rejection category (Audio Quality, Wrong Language, Technical Issue, Content Quality, Incomplete, Other). Evaluate individual speakers with star ratings and optional flagging. Add notes and a PM message. Option to require re-record with same participants.
- **Send To Next Phase** — Add instructions and confirm to advance the session past the issue.

![[pm_review_session_detail.png]]

#### 3. Past View (Completed Sessions)
- Full production timeline with staff names, IDs, roles, dates, and status badges
- Google Drive export location with link
- Individual speaker tracks and final mixed audio

![[past_session_detail.png]]
