The algorithm supports different matching modes depending on the project type:
- **Bilingual Pairing:** Finds two speakers who are both fluent in the same Language Pair (e.g., both speak English and Spanish).
- **Expert-to-Learner:** (If configured) Pairs a Native speaker with a Learner for specific pedagogical data collection.
- **N-Person Grouping:** For 6-person workflows, the algorithm clusters speakers based on shared language commonalities to ensure fluid conversation.
