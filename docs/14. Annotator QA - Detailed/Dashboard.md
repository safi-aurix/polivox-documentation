#### Statistics Cards
- **Total Queue** — All sessions in annotation QA stages
- **Pending QA** — Sessions awaiting first QA review
- **Resubmitted** — Sessions that annotators have fixed and resubmitted
- **Approved** — Sessions that have passed QA and are ready for delivery

#### QA Review Queue
A card shows the top 5 sessions with topic, project, languages, duration, and status (Pending QA or Resubmitted).
