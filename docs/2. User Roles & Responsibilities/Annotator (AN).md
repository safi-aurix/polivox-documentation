Annotators work on transcription and linguistic annotation of approved audio sessions. They produce turn-by-turn transcriptions with speaker identification, language labels, and timing information.

**Responsibilities**
- Transcribe bilingual audio sessions with accurate speaker identification
- Add linguistic annotations: language labels, timing markers, entity tags
- Submit completed annotations for QA review
- Address feedback and resubmit annotations when issues are identified by QA
