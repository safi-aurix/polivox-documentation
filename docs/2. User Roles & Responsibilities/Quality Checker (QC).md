Quality Checkers perform two distinct stages of audio quality review. QC1 reviews raw processed audio for quality issues, while QC2 reviews audio after noise injection to ensure the noise layer hasn't degraded quality.

**Responsibilities**
- Review initial audio recordings for quality issues (QC1): audio levels, clarity, background noise, correct language usage. See [[Initial Processing QA]]
- Review noise-injected audio for quality issues (QC2): noise levels, speech intelligibility, artifact detection. See [[Noise Processing QA]]
- Approve sessions that meet quality standards to advance in the pipeline
- Flag sessions with issues and provide detailed comments for the Media Manager or Project Manager
- Follow QC checklists defined in the project workflow
