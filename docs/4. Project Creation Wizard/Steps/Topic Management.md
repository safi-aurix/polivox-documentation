Projects are organized around specific conversation [[Topics]] to ensure lexical diversity.
- **Topic Input:** PMs add topics (e.g., "Financial Planning," "Travel Logistics").
- Here, languages pairs are assigned by default by system which were added in [[Basic Information]]
	- A language pair can be removed from a specific topic by clicking on that language pair in the topic.

![[step3_topic_management.png]]

Topics can be either added manually or a list can be uploaded from a txt file.

Format of txt file is as follows:

```
[TOPIC] Daily Life & Routines
SCENARIO: Tell me about your typical morning routine. What time do you usually wake up? What's the first thing you do?

[TOPIC] Restaurant Experience
SCENARIO: Role-play ordering food at a restaurant with dietary restrictions. One person is the waiter, the other is a customer with allergies.

[TOPIC] Career Discussion
SCENARIO: Discuss your career path and professional goals for the future. What inspired you to choose your field? Where do you see yourself in 5 years?
```
