A separate dialog for managing the human resources assigned to a project:
- **Speakers Tab** — Search and multi-select speakers from the database. Display shows initials, name, languages, and star rating. Selected speakers shown in a badges list with remove buttons.
- **Staff Tab** — Search and multi-select staff from the database. Display shows initials, name, role (color-coded badge), and star rating. Selected staff shown with role badges and remove buttons.
