The Staff Management page (/staff) mirrors the speaker management interface for staff members.

#### Staff Roles (Color-Coded)
- [[Media Manager (MM)]] (blue badge)
- [[Quality Checker (QC)]] (emerald badge)
- [[Annotator (AN)]] (violet badge)
- [[Annotator QA (AQ)]] (amber badge)

#### CRUD Operations
Identical to speaker management: add (manual or file import), view, edit, delete, flag, and rate. The key difference is that staff entries include a role assignment that determines which workflow nodes they can be assigned to.
