Once a pool of eligible speakers is found, Polivox ranks them using a **Priority Score (S):**

$S=(R×0.6)+(C×0.4)$

Where:
- **R (Rating):** The speaker's average 5-star rating.
- **C (Completion):** A ratio of successfully completed sessions vs. flagged/rejected sessions.
    
**Higher S values are matched first** to ensure that the most reliable speakers handle the bulk of the project's target sessions.
